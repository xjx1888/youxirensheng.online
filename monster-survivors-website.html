About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game that puts you in the middle of a monster apocalypse. Battle against waves of increasingly challenging creatures while collecting power-ups and upgrading your character. With its intuitive controls and engaging gameplay, Monster Survivors offers an unforgettable gaming experience for players of all skill levels.

Game Features

  • Dynamic combat system with multiple weapons and abilities
  • Progressive difficulty with challenging boss battles
  • Character progression and skill tree system
  • Regular updates with new content and features

How to Play

  1. Click the game window to start playing
  2. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  3. Click to shoot and eliminate monsters
  4. Collect power-ups and upgrades to become stronger
  5. Survive as long as possible and achieve high scores
